Bike Sharing

 


Chea Cin is compnay that provides mountain bikes to the general public through an app. The app allows users to remotely rent a mountain bike, by unlocking the bike using a QR code scanner. The user will be billed for their minutes used on the bike. The app allows the user to share the experience with their friends. It uses a map that displays available moun- tain bikes and shows the live location of their friends riding Chea Cin bikes.
